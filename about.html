About Us

At Heart & Hues, we believe that photography is more than just capturing images — it's about freezing emotions, preserving connections, and telling the unique stories that unfold naturally in each moment. Based in Kannur, Kerala, we opened our doors in February 2025 with a simple yet powerful mission: to create timeless, heartfelt images that celebrate the essence of life in all its beauty.

Our approach is all about authenticity. We're not interested in stiff poses or forced smiles. Instead, we focus on the unscripted, the candid, and the real. With a cozy, fine-art style, we capture moments exactly as they are, allowing the true emotions to shine through. Whether it’s a joyful laugh, a tender embrace, or a quiet glance, we believe that these genuine moments are the ones that truly reflect the heart and soul of a person or a story.

We specialize in crafting images that bring warmth and nostalgia, allowing you to relive the magic of your most cherished memories. Each photograph we create is not just an image but a timeless reminder of the connections, laughter, and love that make life so special. Our goal is to give you photographs that evoke happiness and allow you to cherish these precious moments for years to come.

We are so grateful for the opportunity to share in these meaningful moments with you, and we can't wait to capture your story. From the heart of Kannur, Kerala, Heart & Hues is here to help you preserve your memories in a way that is as beautiful and unique as you are.

Meet Our Founder


Heart & Hues was founded by Mr. Sishil Sushanth, a passionate photographer dedicated to capturing authentic and heartfelt moments. With a vision to bring warmth, nostalgia, and a sense of connection through photography, Sishil has turned this dream into a reality.
